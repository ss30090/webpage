Freshly Fried Crispy Chicken Nuggets

The main ingredients of freshly fried crispy chicken nuggets are skinless chicken breasts and chicken thighs. The chicken breasts and chicken thighs are beaten through a grinding blade and ground into a chicken mass to make a perfect chicken nugget.

Taste: original flavor (without any added spices), salt and pepper, chili powder, tomato sauce, homemade sweet and sour sauce, BBQ sauce

Crispy and Juicy Fried Chicken Drumsticks

The main ingredient of crispy and juicy fried chicken drumsticks is organic chicken drumsticks soaked in various marinades and rubbed. In order to allow the chicken to penetrate the marinade, add cornstarch to deep-fry the perfect juicy fried chicken drumsticks.

Taste: original, salt and pepper, spicy

Crispy Vitality Fried Chicken Wings

The main ingredients of crispy vigorous fried chicken wings are the same as fried chicken legs.

Taste: original, salt and pepper, spicy
